# Berserk-Cata-DDA
"Berserk" is a dark mod for Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead, inspired by the manga by Kantaro Miura. Adds to the game: Legendary swords of Gats,  Zodd's Sword is a cursed Berserker armor with a power bonus due to some debuff of two professions — a harsh Berserker warrior and his fan with a copy of the sword, as well as a FULL-fledged Nosferatu demon Zod
